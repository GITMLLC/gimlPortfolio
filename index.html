About

Guardian IT Management LLC

Guardian IT Management LLC is a dedicated team of Oracle Financials functional consultants with a proven track record of successful implementations and optimizations. Our core expertise lies in Oracle Cloud Financials, where we hold certifications in key modules such as General Ledger, Revenue Management, Account Hub, Receivables, and Payables.


Our team brings over 17 years of collective experience in the Oracle ERP landscape, with more than 10 years specifically focused on Oracle Cloud technologies. This extensive experience allows us to understand the complexities of various industries, including Oil & Gas, Higher Education, High Tech Industrial Manufacturing, Consumer Goods Manufacturing, Public Sector, Healthcare, and Retail Business.

Services

Our Oracle Financials Services

Guardian IT Management LLC offers a comprehensive suite of Oracle Financials consulting services designed to help you maximize your investment and achieve your business objectives. Our key service areas include:

  • Oracle Cloud Financials Implementation

    We provide end-to-end implementation services for Oracle Cloud Financials, ensuring a smooth and efficient transition. This includes project management, solution design, system configuration, testing, and go-live support.

  • Data Conversions and Integrations (RICE)

    Our expertise in RICE (Reports, Interfaces, Conversions, Extensions) ensures seamless data migration from legacy systems and robust integrations with other critical business applications.

  • System Configuration and Optimization

    We help you configure and optimize your Oracle Financials system to align with your specific business processes and reporting requirements. This includes Chart of Accounts design, enterprise structure setup, and workflow optimization.

  • Specialized Expertise

    • General Ledger
    • Account Payable(AP)
    • Account Receivables(AR)
    • Fixed Assets(FA)
    • Cash Management(CE)
    • Intercompany
    • Labor Distribution
    • Revenue Management
    • Account Hub

  • Customized Reporting and Analytics

    We develop tailored reports and analytics solutions to provide you with valuable insights into your financial performance.

  • Business Process Refinement

    We analyze your existing business processes and recommend improvements to enhance efficiency, accuracy, and compliance.

  • Upgrades and Migrations

    We assist with seamless upgrades to the latest Oracle Cloud releases and migrations from on-premise systems.

  • Training and Support

    We provide comprehensive training to your team and ongoing support to ensure the continued success of your Oracle Financials system.
